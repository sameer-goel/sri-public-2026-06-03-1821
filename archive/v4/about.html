Our Story

The Story of Om Sacred Space

How a life lived all in, with its bold leaps and unlikely turns, led to a single dot at the center of the Sri Yantra, and the sacred space that was born from it.

Meet the Founder

The All-In Type

Om Sacred Space was founded by Laura, known in her healing work as Suhana. From early on, she tried everything the moment she got the chance. There were the "normal" experiments, and then there were the ones that were not so normal at all.

On the ordinary side: printing custom quotes onto t-shirts and mugs, writing children's books, and teaching music from the age of fourteen. Things that grow quiet with time, but leave you with the experience and the skills all the same.

On the other side were the leaps that took her right to the edge. A "hostess" job abroad that nearly turned into something far darker, and a protection she can only describe as Divine. An article written for the wrong kind of people. A fast, risky bet that, thank God, never arrived at her door. Moving in with a man she had known for only two months, in the middle of a lockdown. And walking away, twice, from a conservatory that felt too toxic to stay in.

Whatever the chapter, the pattern was always the same: all in. The wildest stories she still only tells out loud. One never knows.

A Restless Calling

From Boredom to a Knock on London's Door

One adventure led to another until she landed at a small private music label. It was the first truly satisfying job she had tasted: fair pay, and work inside the worlds she loved most, music and writing.

But for a personality like hers, every restriction became an invitation to rebel. Within a year, the boundaries felt too narrow and the lack of freedom turned to boredom. It didn't last, and it didn't end gently.

So she flew to London and literally knocked on doors, hoping for a once-in-a-lifetime yes. The yes finally came months later, by which point she had already changed her mind.

A Single Dot

The Picture That Opened a Dimension

In the waiting, she gave sound healing a try, through the most expensive and probably least valuable course out there. Nearly everything it taught could already be found on the internet, long before the AI boom.

Yet tucked inside its workbook was an image that lit a curiosity she had never felt before: the Sri Yantra, the sacred geometry said to transform your life simply by being gazed upon.

The irony was not lost on her, that she came to the Divine chasing a short-term promise of success. The moment her eyes locked onto the central dot, a new dimension of reality opened. A multiplier of perspectives. After hours of stillness, a total expansion of awareness.

The Birth of a Sacred Space

Born From the Center of the Geometry

Om Sacred Space was born from that single dot placed at the middle of the geometries. The dot was the stillness of the other side; she was the part of the play holding the connection from this side. The sacred space was what arose between them, the meeting of energies, the engagement of duality, and in time, the mastery of it.

With no studies, no job, and no children to anchor her, she kept one commitment above all others: to return to the dot in the Sri Yantra every single day, no matter what. She kept playing with instruments, experimenting with sound, and finding her way into trance and beyond, one step at a time.

Soon, meditation stopped being comfortable and became the real adventure: facing her own mental limitations and leaping past them. There is nothing more daring than jumping off the cliff of the mind into the dark depths of stillness. Surprise, surprise, it never got boring.

The Gifts

What the Quiet Work Gave Back

Slowly, every part of her life began to reflect the work happening behind closed doors. Small sound baths in studios grew into workshop events in open spaces. One-on-one sessions started revealing small miracles.

A lifelong shyness dissolved, her heart opened wide to the world, and her relationships deepened with it. She became someone who not only dreams and plans, but acts, with a sense of responsibility that reached far beyond the "I."

The journeys through duality and what lies beyond it brought many gifts, among them the greatest so far: the expanded ability of vision, and the Divine Light.

Where We Are Going

A Sanctuary in the Making

Om Sacred Space is in constant development. The closest dream is a sanctuary, a physical place to gather, a retreat where a simple dot in the Sri Yantra and a commitment to ourselves can change lives for the better on this planet.
