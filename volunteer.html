Help Us Grow a Self-Sustaining Sacred Home

We are building a physical retreat that grows its own food, makes its own clean energy, and turns its own waste back into life. If you are a grower, a builder, an off-grid maker, or an entrepreneur already working on ideas like these, this is a real place to put them into the ground.

Come Build With Us

Our Invitation

A Living Laboratory for Your Ideas

Om Sacred Space is becoming a real, physical home: a retreat designed to feed itself, power itself, and regenerate the land it sits on. We are looking for the people who already know how to make that happen.

Are you already building the future of regenerative living, and looking for a place to put it into practice?

So many brilliant people are working on the pieces of a self-sufficient life: growing food with permaculture, generating clean off-grid electricity, turning organic waste into biogas, building naturally, harvesting water. Often the missing piece is a real site and a committed community to bring it all together in one living system.

That is what we are offering. Not just a request for help, but ground to build on, a founding circle to build with, and a sanctuary where your work is used and honoured every single day. You prototype, you teach, you leave something that keeps giving long after the build.

If you are a grower, an engineer, a natural builder, a circular-systems thinker, or an entrepreneur with a solution that deserves a home, we would love to meet you. Tell us what you are building. It is easy to start, and below is exactly what we are looking for.

Who We're Looking For

The Skills That Build a Home

These are the hands and minds that turn a piece of land into a thriving, self-sustaining sanctuary. See yourself in one of these? We'd love to hear from you.

Grow the Food

Permaculture designers and regenerative growers: food forests, crops, soil building, seed-saving, and gardens that feed a community year-round.

Power the Home

Off-grid energy makers: solar, micro-hydro, wind, and battery storage. Help us generate clean, independent electricity for the whole space.

Close the Loop

Waste-to-resource innovators: composting, recycling, and turning organic waste into biogas and rich soil. Nothing wasted, everything renewed.

Build by Hand

Natural builders and makers: earth and timber construction, rainwater harvesting, greywater, and the practical infrastructure a living home needs.

Bring Your Innovation

Eco-entrepreneurs already working on regenerative ideas: this is a real site to pilot, prove, and scale what you're building. Bring your project.

Hold the Space

Community builders, teachers, cooks, storytellers, and connectors: keep the human heart of the sanctuary warm, and help gather the right circle.

It's Easy to Start

Tell Us What You're Building

No long forms and no commitment. Just your name, what you do, and a link to your work if you have one. We read every message and reply personally.

Whether you already run a regenerative project or simply have the skills and the will, you're welcome here. Share what you're working on and let's explore building it together.
